## Core Principles

### 1. Risk Management First
- Never invest more than you can lose
- Start small, learn, then scale
- Understand before you trade

### 2. Fee Awareness
- Every trade has costs
- Maker vs Taker fees
- Funding rates in futures

### 3. Security Priority
- 2FA always
- Cold storage for holdings
- Never share keys/seeds
